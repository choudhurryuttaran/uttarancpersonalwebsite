Teaching

I have taught a variety of mathematics courses ranging from Foundations of Mathematics to Calculus. At the University of Memphis, I taught a Foundations of Mathematics class with 80 students while maintaining high engagement and motivation throughout the semester.

I have experience working with students from diverse financial and socio-economic backgrounds, and I incorporate real-life examples, humor, and multimedia into my teaching to keep classes engaging.

I also have experience tutoring students one-on-one, focusing on individualized problem-solving strategies.
